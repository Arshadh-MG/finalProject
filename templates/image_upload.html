How It Works
  1. Upload an image containing animals (domestic or wild)
  2. The system uses YOLOv8 to detect and identify animals in the image
  3. Each detected animal is analyzed for injury indicators such as:
    • Abnormal posture or body collapse
    • Visible wounds or injuries
    • Asymmetrical stance
  4. If injury probability exceeds 75%, an alert is generated
