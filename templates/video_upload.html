How Video Analysis Works
  1. Upload a video file containing animals in motion
  2. The system extracts frames and runs YOLOv8 detection on each frame
  3. Animals are tracked across frames using the SlowFast Network
  4. Behavioral analysis identifies injury indicators such as:
    • Limping or uneven gait
    • Leg dragging or abnormal movement
    • Sudden collapse or prolonged immobility
    • Distress movement patterns
  5. Evidence clips are saved when injury is detected
  6. Alerts are generated when injury probability exceeds 75%
